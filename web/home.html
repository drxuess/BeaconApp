How does it work?

add_alert

Sign up for alerts in your area

Sign up with your phone number and postcode to start customising Beacon for you.

place

We figure out when there is a fire near you

Our data, algorithm and input from other Beacon users identify wild fires that are near your postcode. We also calculate the distance, severity and movement of the fire.

sms

You're alerted by text message

If there is a fire threat near your postcode, we will send you a text message and a link to the necessary information for you to make an informed and timely decision.
